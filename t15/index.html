About T20 Cricket

T20 (Twenty20) cricket is a dynamic, fast-paced format where each team plays a single innings of 20 overs. Loved for its explosive batting, strategic bowling, and thrilling finishes, T20 matches deliver excitement in just a few hours. The format has revolutionized cricket, bringing together top international players, vibrant leagues, and passionate fans worldwide.

  • 20 overs per side
  • Big hits, quick runs, thrilling finishes
  • Global tournaments & leagues
